# Steal the honeycomb

## How to play

- WASD - move
- Mouse - turn and look around
- Space - Jump

## Story

You've been eating some porridge. It's not near as delicious as you'd hoped. It needs honey. bad. You recall there's a bee hive just off to the side of your home. Your goal is to bring some honey home to mix into your porridge.

## Win

You win if you bring home honey.

## Lose

You lose if you get stung by a bee or mauled by a bear.

## Playtime

2 to 3 minutes.
