# Playtesting Question ideas
### 20260317
Goal of the player? are they behaving in a way they are supposed to?

I want the player to solve the following puzzles.
1. Figure out the first thing they need to acquire. which is honey.
2. Acquire that honey.
3. Figure out how to evade the bee. Bee's don't like water/rivers.
4. Realize they can no longer get back to their home. Figure out how to get home after they accidently release the bear.

How I am guiding them currently.
1. Locking them into a small space.
2. Making the collectable honey actor out by looking different than anything else in the game.

Things I'm not sure about.
1. How I should solve the issue if they are not being currently being guided well. I'm not sure how to convey that the player wants honey without using text or narrative audio.
2. If it's too short.
